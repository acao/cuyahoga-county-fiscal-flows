Key Insight: Fiscal Transfer Mechanism

Cleveland's 2.5% income tax on all wages earned in the city creates a significant fiscal transfer from suburban residents who work in Cleveland but live elsewhere. This generates revenue for Cleveland while reducing pressure on suburban tax rates.

Policy Recommendations & Regional Solutions

1. Regional Tax Sharing Mechanism

Implement a Cuyahoga County Regional Tax Sharing System modeled on the Twin Cities Fiscal Disparities Program, sharing 25% of new commercial property tax growth above baseline.

2. Transportation Investment Rebalancing

Prioritize transit routes serving >65% cross-municipal commuter flow and implement regional transportation impact fees for developments generating significant cross-border traffic.

3. Economic Development Coordination

Establish a County Economic Development Compact to coordinate incentive packages and eliminate destructive inter-municipal competition, saving an estimated $23 million annually.
